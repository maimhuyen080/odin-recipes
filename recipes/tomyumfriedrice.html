Tom Yum Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp finely chopped galangal

  • 1 lemongrass, pale part bruised and finely chopped

  • 3 birdseye chillies

  • 4 makrut lime leaves, finely chopped

  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil

  • 1 small onion, diced

  • 10 small peeled and deveined prawns

  • 2 eggs

  • 1 small tomato, cut into wedges

  • 3 cups day-old cooked rice

  • cucumber slices

  • spring onion

  • lime wedges

  • 2 tbsp nam prik pao

  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

  • 1 tbsp fish sauce

  • 1 tbsp sugar

  • 2 tbsp lime juice

Steps

  1. Use a mortar and pestle to pound the galangal and lemongrass to a fine paste.

    Then add the chillies and pound again until you reach a fairly fine consistency.

    Then add the makrut limes leaves and pound until bruised. Set aside for later.

  2. For the stir-fry sauce, mix the ingredients in a small bowl. Set aside for later.

  3. Heat the large work or pan over medium heat, drizzle 2-3 tbsp of oil and stir fry the garlic

    and shallots for a few seconds with fragrant. Next, add the prawns and cook for 1-2 minutes.

    Sprinkle a pinch of salt to taste. Add chillies and stir it well for a few seconds.

  4. Add cooked rice and pour the sauce mixture over the rice. Bring the heat to a hig and

    stir fry for about 2 mins.

  5. Finish with spring onions, and toss everything well. Transfer to a serving bowl and

    garnish with, cucumber, chillies, and lime wedges, adn coriander.
